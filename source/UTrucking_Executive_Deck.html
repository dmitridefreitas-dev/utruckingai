The Opportunity

The front desk fields the same calls, all season long

  • "Where's my pickup?" · "What's my order status?" · "How much is storage?" — the same questions, thousands of times.
  • Call volume spikes at move-out and move-in — exactly when the team is busiest.
  • UTrucking serves roughly half of WashU undergrads, so the repeat-call load is real.
Nearly every one of those calls can be answered automatically.

The Solution

A friendly voice that never puts anyone on hold

Trust & Privacy

Built to protect student data

  • Confirms identity before sharing anything — the caller's name plus a second detail.
  • Never reveals one student's information to another, even on a close name match.
  • Safety guardrails keep it on-task and on-brand.
  • Stress-tested against wrong-name and impostor scenarios.
